Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about working with me

What is your design process like?

+

My design process begins with understanding your vision and needs. We'll have an initial consultation, followed by concept development, design refinement, and final implementation.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Interior design projects typically take 6-12 weeks, while branding projects usually take 4-8 weeks from start to finish.

Do you work with clients outside of London?

+

Yes! While I'm based in London, I work with clients throughout the UK and internationally. Virtual consultations and project management make distance collaboration seamless.

What's included in your design packages?

+

Each package is tailored to your needs but typically includes initial consultation, concept development, detailed design plans, material specifications, and ongoing support throughout implementation.
